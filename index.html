Sensing Solutions

At TOBI Technologies, we engineer cutting-edge sensing solutions, from advanced mobile applications to bespoke hardware prototypes. Human-Factor Studies

At the core of many of our innovations are human-factor studies. Whether we serve end users who harness our technology or humans being integral to the sensory task itself, we are proud to offer studies centred around humans, ensuring that our technology integrates seamlessly and resonates deeply with human experiences.

Discovery and Analysis


Complex problems require an intricate understanding of all variables and parameters. We work closely with our project partners to carefully analyze the problem. Expanding upon the state-of-the-art, we find solutions that break that status-quo.
